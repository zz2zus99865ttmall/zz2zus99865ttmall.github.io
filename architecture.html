Palace of Justice

Architecturally valuable buildings

This majestic building is of modern architecture and was built by architect Roberto Londoño. It houses the Supreme Court of Justice, the Constitutional Court, the Council of State and the Superior Council of the Judiciary. At the entrance is the phrase of General Francisco de Paula Santander: "Colombians the weapons have given you independence, the laws will give you freedom."

Continental

Old hotel de Bogotá

Rebuilt by Coninsa Ramón H. and the Blue Door hotel chain. It emulates the modernity that the owners of the old building gave it in its golden age. The Continental was the hotel of choice for illustrious visitors from high society. Located in the center of the city, it dazzles you with its giant light sign, transporting you to an ancient time, perhaps its most brilliant era.
