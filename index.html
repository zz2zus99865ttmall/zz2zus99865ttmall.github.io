    VANAAL

NOSTOS

In the vast canvas of the forest, nature is the art that inspires the wandering soul's journey. Among the trees and stars in the firmament I discover the secrets that weave the melancholy of the heart. And art becomes the return of the longed-for hope. The return to myself.

WEAVING STORIES THROUGH A LENS SO THEY

don't escape the imagination

ART & NATURE

Art is the sublime echo of the reflection of human life. In my artwork, I seek to capture the essence of nature to reveal the beauty that dwells within me.

BEGINNINGS

In the solitude of the mysterious woods, among shadows and rustling leaves, I began to capture my nakedness, as an echo of my own inner search, to understand myself and at the same time to create art out of the ordinary. I traveled through different forests in my country, making ephemeral moments moments to be immortalized.

TRAVELING EXPLORING CAPTURING EMOTIONS

to understand in images what my mind processes

FOUR ELEMENTS

In a moment when melancholy clouded my days, a fortuitous encounter with photography, spirituality, nature and me, I found the serenity I thought I had lost, in a communion between art, the universe and my soul, restoring harmony in my being.

INTROS PECTION

Look within yourself to find the art. Allow yourself to discover the vast canvas within you.

ART AS THERAPY

I found a refuge where the whispers of pain dissolve in the harmony of creation. Amidst beautiful forests and with each shot of the camera, the pain is set free, and one enters a world where judgments do not exist, only the liberation of being.

IT'S YOUR TURN

Let your heart and your ancestors guide you. Let go of all the pain, the tears, the fears and the judgments. Reconnect with the spirits of the forest and let your imagination soar. It's time to embark on a journey. It's time to create art.
